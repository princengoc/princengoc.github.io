Ngoc Mai Tran

Assistant Professor

I am an assistant professor in Mathematics at UT Austin and a Bonn Research Fellow at the Hausdorff Center for Mathematics.

Previously I was a Bonn Junior Fellow (2015-2017), a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow (2013 - 2015) and a Vietnam Education Foundation Fellow (2009-2013).

I am a data-driven mathematician. My research lies at the intersection of tropical geometry, probability, combinatorics, economics and neuroscience.
